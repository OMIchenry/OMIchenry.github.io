Regime-Aware Equity Selection Strategy

Built a market regime classifier to identify risk-on vs. risk-off conditions using momentum breadth, volatility, and lagged returns. Conditioned equity selection on regime signals.

Constructed equal-weighted long portfolios by ranking stocks on predicted upside probabilities within each regime. Evaluated out-of-sample performance versus SPY across turnover, transaction cost, drawdown, and risk-adjusted return metrics.

Pairs Trading Engine

A full statistical arbitrage backtesting platform. Select from 6 cointegrated equity pairs, run Engle-Granger cointegration tests, compute dynamic hedge ratios via OLS, and generate z-score entry/exit signals.

Features a complete backtest engine with configurable lookback window, entry/exit/stop-loss z-score thresholds, and transaction costs in bps. Outputs Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, win rate, and profit factor. Four interactive canvas charts: spread & z-score with trade markers, equity curve with drawdown shading, normalized price series, and z-score distribution with normal fit overlay. Trade log with full event history.
